IN HOUSEFACILITIES

Sampling

We have in-house sampling co-ordinators & masters who are experienced and technically sound, in order to keep up with the ever evolving fashion trends. We have an in-house showroom to showcase our product range to our clients.


Production & finishing

Working on assembly line production, there are four machine chains each handled by a qualified line supervisor and specifications checker. In all there are 500 machines along with the required supplementary machines . We have a well equipped and clean washing & finishing unit that finishes and packs 5000 pcs a day.


Fabric stores

A very wide array of fabrics are displayed in our stores showcasing all types of latest fabrics for our clients to choose from for their sampling and also there are stock fabrics available for smaller production runs.


R&D

There is an extremely conscious effort right from the top management down the line in all of the work force to maintain high and consistent quality standards. At all the stages of production there are stringent systems that are followed to keep quality under check. Inspections are done at greige fabric, processed fabric, cutting, stitching, finishing and packing stages to ensure a high quality final garment.

TIME LEAD

Depending upon the fabric processes and value addition to the garment we deliver from 60 to 90 days after the confirmation of order
